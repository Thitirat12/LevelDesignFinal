Grungy Wall Tileset with Torch Animation (Micro-Pack) [32x32]
by Hypnobius
---

Thank you for supporting me by downloading this asset pack! 


CONTENTS:
- 1 tilesheet (PNG)
- 20 total tiles [32x32], including:
    - 4 torch animation frames
    - 16 modular wall variations (including corners and edges)
- Tileset is best suited for platformers or side-scrolling games.
- 2 additional fully-lit tile variations can be found on the bottom-left corner of the tilesheet
- Torch background tiles are on the bottom right of the tilesheet


TILESET DETAILS:
- Tile size: 32x32 pixels
- File format: PNG with transparency
- Vector-based and fully scalable (demos are shown at 3x scale)
- Background color for this pack:
    - HEX: '#162b1f'
    - RGB: (22, 43, 31)


USAGE TIPS:
- Tiles are modular and designed to align seamlessly in Tiled or custom engines.
- Torch-lit tiles (bottom right, tile #19 and #20) are intended to be placed behind (18) and above (19) the torch animation.
- For best results, use this pack over the background fill color or a similar hue.
